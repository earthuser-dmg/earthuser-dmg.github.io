About Me

Product designer focused on craft, storytelling, and strategy at the intersection of digital design and consumer devices. Currently at Snap Lab, working on fun projects for Spectacles. Previously, worked on Monetization at Meta and the Alexa App and Echo Show suite at Amazon. Before diving into tech, I studied Architecture and worked on professional projects ranging from skyscrapers to masterplans. My work aims to bridge the gap between the digital and physical worlds, one thoughtful interaction at a time.
