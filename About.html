About Me

I’m a UX & Product Designer specialized in end-to-end design. I’m passionate about design systems and tackling complex problems—those are the parts of design that make me lose track of time.

Over the past 3 years, I’ve worked with Decathlon and Alibaba, covering B2B, B2C, mobile, and web. At Alibaba, I was in charge of flight products on the digital travel platform; at Decathlon, I’ve been bridging production with digital transformation. Along the way, I’ve picked up a pretty diverse skill tree—motion design, video editing, qualitative research, and service design—because why stop at one tool when you can have the whole toolkit?

I got into design back in high school, when CMF and software design first blew my mind (before I even knew the term HCI). That mix of curiosity, a problem-solving brain, and a streak of perfectionism made it feel like I’d found my thing.

Outside of work, I’m a coffee enthusiast to a slightly ridiculous degree, always chasing that perfect cup. I have a ginger cat (you might have seen him playing on my homepage) and I love painting, jogging, and live music. All of these hobbies feed back into my design—they keep my eyes tuned to beauty and my mind open to new ideas.
